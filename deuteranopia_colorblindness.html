Red-green Colorblindness

Deuteranopia is a red-green defect. Persons with red-green defects have difficulty distinguishing between reds, greens, and yellows but can discriminate between blues and yellows (2). Deuteranopia is caused by the absence of green cones. The results are similar to those of protanopia, but the dimming is not as apparent (5).
