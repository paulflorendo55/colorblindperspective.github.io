"This was instrumental for me because I can better distinguish colors in warm tones as compared to cool tones."

This was one of my architectural design plates in my second-year. The brief was all about making a food kiosk that featured Philippine vernacular architecture, as well as a PWD-friendly bathroom. For this output, I used Copic Colored Markers. Most of the colors are in warm tones. This was instrumental for me because I can better distinguish colors in warm tones as compared to cool tones. Also, Philippine Architecture is predominantly portrayed to have warm colors, apparently due to its setting in a tropical climate.

Esquisse Plate Title: A Proposed Filipino-Themed Food Park Kiosk
